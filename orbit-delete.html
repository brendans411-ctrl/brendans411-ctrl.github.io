Account & Data Deletion Request

What data we store

When you play ORBIT and sign in with Google or create a guest username, we store the following data in Firebase Realtime Database:

  • Your chosen display name (pilot name)
  • Your personal best score (longest orbit time in seconds)
  • A unique player ID (Firebase UID for Google sign-in, or a randomly generated ID for guests)
  • The timestamp of your last score submission

We do not store passwords, payment information, location data, or any other personal information beyond what is listed above.

How to request deletion

1
Send an email to brendan@xpresslogistics.com.au with the subject line ORBIT Data Deletion Request
2
Include your pilot name (the name shown on the leaderboard) and/or the Google account email you used to sign in
3
We will delete your data within 7 days and confirm by reply email
EMAIL DELETION REQUEST

Data retention after deletion

Data type Deleted when? Notes
Pilot name Within 7 days of request Removed from leaderboard immediately
Score / personal best Within 7 days of request Removed from all leaderboards
Player ID Within 7 days of request Firebase UID or guest ID deleted
Local device data Immediately on app uninstall Stored in device localStorage only

Note: If you signed in with Google, deleting your ORBIT data does not affect your Google account in any way.

Questions

If you have any questions about your data or this process, contact us at brendan@xpresslogistics.com.au
