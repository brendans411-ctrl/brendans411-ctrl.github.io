ORBIT

Privacy Policy · Last updated July 2026

Overview

ORBIT is a mobile game developed by Brendan Shannon. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the ORBIT app on Android.

By playing ORBIT, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Information we collect

We collect the following information when you use ORBIT:

  • Pilot name — the display name you choose when you first launch the game, used to identify you on the global leaderboard
  • Game scores — your personal best survival time, stored to power the leaderboard
  • Player ID — a unique identifier (your Firebase UID if you sign in with Google, or a randomly generated ID for guest players)
  • Score timestamp — the date and time of your last score submission
  • Google account information — if you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your Google display name and account ID via Firebase Authentication. We do not receive your password or payment information.
  • Crash logs and app performance data — collected automatically by Firebase to help us identify and fix bugs

We do not collect location data, contacts, photos, microphone input, or any other sensitive personal information.

How we use your information

  • To display your name and score on the global leaderboard
  • To save your personal best score across sessions
  • To authenticate you when you sign in with Google
  • To monitor app performance and fix crashes

We do not sell your data, use it for targeted advertising, or share it with third parties except as described below.

Third-party services

ORBIT uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase — used for authentication, real-time database (leaderboard scores), and crash reporting. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — used to serve banner and interstitial ads within the game. AdMob may collect device information and advertising IDs to serve ads. Google Privacy Policy

You can opt out of personalised ads by adjusting your device's ad settings under Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.

Data storage and security

Your leaderboard data is stored in Google Firebase Realtime Database, hosted in Singapore (asia-southeast1). Firebase uses industry-standard encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.

Your local personal best score is stored on your device only and is not transmitted unless you have submitted a score to the leaderboard.

Data retention

We retain your leaderboard data (name, score, player ID) for as long as you are an active player. If you request deletion, your data will be removed within 7 days.

Local device data is deleted automatically when you uninstall the app.

Children's privacy

ORBIT is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Play as a guest without providing any personal information

To request access or deletion of your data, visit our Data Deletion page or contact us directly.
